Welcome

Welcome to Congregation B'nai Israel, a vibrant Reform Jewish community in Bridgeport, Connecticut. We are committed to spiritual growth, lifelong learning, and social justice. Join us in celebrating Jewish life through worship, education, and community engagement.

Jewish Life & Learning

From our Bonim Preschool to adult education programs, we offer opportunities for learning and connection at every stage of life. Our youth programs, Hebrew school, and high school initiatives foster a strong Jewish identity and community involvement.

Tikkun Olam

We are deeply committed to repairing the world through acts of social justice and community service. Join our efforts in food drives, blood donations, refugee support, and interfaith partnerships that make a meaningful impact locally and globally.

Clergy & Staff

Meet our dedicated clergy and staff, including Rabbi Evan Schultz and Rabbi Sarah Marion. Their leadership, compassion, and wisdom guide our congregation in worship, education, and community building.
